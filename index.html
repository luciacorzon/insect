Butterflies

The most colorful bugs

Butterflies are famous for their colorful and beautiful wings.

Thanks to their life cycle, starting as worm and transforming into a butterfly, they represent rebirth.



Some interesting facts about butterflies
  • Some butterfly species migrate from the cold.
  • Butterflies actually have four wings, not two.
  • There are almost 20,000 butterfly species, the most common ones are:
    • Monarch
    • Painted lady
    • Viceroy

The life cycle of a butterfly is very special. Here are the four stages of a butterfly's life:
  1. Egg stage: the eggs are laid during summer, spring or fall.
  2. Caterpillar: the feeding stage. The larva eats and eats so it can grow bigger.
  3. Pupa: the transition stage. The transition from caterpillar to butterfly begins. It happens inside the chrysalis. It is very slow.
  4. Adult: reproductive stage. The final form is reached and now the main purpose of this interesting insect is to survive and reproduce.
